Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your BottleScan account and associated data.

You can delete your BottleScan account at any time from directly within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes your account and the bottle data associated with it. This action cannot be undone.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the BottleScan app on your phone.
  2. Sign in to your account if you are not already authenticated.
  3. Go to the Settings page.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm when prompted.

Heads up: Deleting your account is permanent. Once confirmed, your account and its bottle data are removed and cannot be recovered.
